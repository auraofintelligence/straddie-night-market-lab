# Onboarding Fields

The market onboarding form should collect useful common data from anyone who may be involved in a market.

## Applicant

- Name, business or group.
- Participant type.
- Private follow-up contact.
- Public listing contact, if approved.
- Public listing permission.

## Market Fit

- Preferred market, venue or event.
- Date, season or availability.
- Village or area.
- Host or organiser, if known.
- Next step: contact applicant, match with organiser, check with venue, request documents or keep private.

## Stall Or Offer

- What the person sells, shows, serves or does.
- Category.
- Product photos, website or social links.
- Stall layout or presentation.
- Stall size.
- Site needs: table, chair, shelter, power, lighting, water, bins, vehicle access, EFTPOS reception or hanging/screen space.

## Required Checks

- Public liability insurance.
- Food licence or food-safety details where relevant.
- Food, gas or electrical notes.
- Venue, organiser, cultural, media, music, image or sponsor review notes.

## Public Information

- Public listing draft.
- Visitor FAQ: times, cost, booking, meals, toilets, accessibility, wet weather, cash/card, parking, ferry, bus and last return.
- Expiry or review date for any public notice.
