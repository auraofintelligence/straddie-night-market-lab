# Partner Map

This is a starter map for conversations. Partner status begins at contact planning and moves forward through direct review.

## Venue Partners

- Point Lookout Bowls Club: existing market pattern and family/community market baseline.
- Little Ship Club: Dunwich waterfront, sunset and ferry-adjacent tests.
- The Straddie Hotel: late trade, live music, Sunday sessions and visitor concentration.
- Amity Point Community Club: Amity community music and family gathering potential.
- Point Lookout Community Hall: arts, projection and wet-weather style gatherings.
- Straddie Sharks / Ron Stark Oval: sports, family and community-event layer.

## Transport Partners

- SeaLink: vehicle ferry, passenger ferry, terminal notices, visitor-flow questions.
- Gold Cats / Stradbroke Flyer: water taxi, One Mile Jetty, mainland bus link and no-booking walk-up behaviour.
- Stradbroke Island Buses: ferry to Point Lookout and Amity route fit.
- Venue courtesy buses and local transfer options: safe movement after dark.

## Data and Distribution Partners

- Straddie Noticeboard Network: public-safe notices, expiry dates, QR questions, venue-specific feeds.
- Straddie Media / news network: gig guide, weekend wrap, ferry-screen updates, local beat routing and post-event learning.
- Ready S.E.T. Co-op + Hyperlocal Media: training, help desk, local content rhythm, public proof and media skills pathway.
- Shared Table Initiative: food sharing, surplus rescue, prep-space questions and evidence.
- Sovereign Space Builder: real-place mapping before big-system promises.
- Virtual Minjerribah / future simulation layer: visitor-flow, ferry choice, crowd comfort, weather and projection assumptions.
- Stradbroke Grants Lab: entity search, future evidence packs, milestones and acquittals.

## First Ask

Can we map what already happens and what each partner would need before a tiny pilot is even proposed?

## Better First Asks

- Ask what each partner already knows.
- Ask what would make their job easier.
- Ask which data can be shared in aggregate.
- Ask which details need verification before public noticeboard use.
- Search the Grants Lab entities list before outreach.
- Route confirmed public stories through Ready S.E.T. Media when useful.
