# Question Pattern

The night market lab should create better questions before it creates public claims.

## First Question

Could a market layer help this place or event, or would it add noise?

## Venue

- Who can say yes?
- Who must be consulted?
- Who might be affected?
- What already happens here?
- What is the safe capacity before it stops feeling good?
- Which parts of the site are public, private, culturally sensitive or operational?

## Market Layer

- Is this one Strange But True stall, a maker row, a food layer, a projection corner or a distributed trail?
- Who books stalls?
- What data does the organiser already collect?
- What do stallholders need: space, table, shade, power, lighting, storage, water, public notice, payment options?
- What local benefit should be visible before visitors are invited?

## Light Projection

- What surface is available?
- Who owns or controls that surface?
- How bright can it be while respecting neighbours, wildlife, drivers and venue operations?
- Does any image, language, song, place-name or story need cultural review?
- Is power safe, weather-protected and easy to pack down?

## Shared Table

- Is food part of this test, optional, or outside scope?
- Is it a snack stall, shared meal, food rescue, produce swap, pantry call-out or no-food night?
- What needs cold storage, labelling, allergy care, food handling and waste management?
- What surplus can be safely shared after the event?
- What evidence helps a grant or partner report while protecting private participation?

## Transport

- Which ferry or water taxi choices fit the proposed time?
- What is the last safe return option?
- Is the venue ferry-adjacent, bus-linked, courtesy-bus linked, walkable or car-dependent?
- Could partner messaging spread arrivals across calmer windows?
- What aggregate visitor-flow data is useful with privacy kept intact?

## Noticeboard

- What can be public now?
- What must wait for verification?
- Who can update cancellations, venue changes or weather notes?
- When does the notice expire?
- Which QR question would create the most useful next piece of data?
