# Shared Table Bridge

The Shared Table is associated infrastructure for the night market lab.

It should be treated as a small, permissioned layer that can travel with a market candidate when food, surplus, prep space, local growers, shared meals or community evidence are relevant.

## Relationship To The Night Market

The night market asks:

- Could people gather here?
- Could stalls fit here?
- Could light projection fit here?
- Could visitors arrive and leave well?

The Shared Table adds:

- Could food be gathered, checked, shared or saved from waste here?
- Could a safe prep or storage area be mapped?
- Could a public call-out help while protecting private people?
- Could the outcome create useful evidence for a future grant or partner conversation?

## Relationship To Sovereign Space Builder

The Sovereign Space Builder bridge says: map the real places before promising the big system.

For this repo, that means:

- Map tables, prep zones, bins, water, storage, power, paths and exits.
- Attach safety notes, photos and logs only where appropriate.
- Mark private, culturally restricted or sensitive locations.
- Export public-safe evidence and keep raw private data out of public feeds.

## First Pilot Shape

Use a tiny pilot:

- One host location.
- One simple food role.
- One public notice.
- One private roster.
- One evidence note after pack-down.

## Public Boundary

Public feeds should exclude:

- Private participant details.
- Sensitive cultural locations or stories.
- Unverified food-safety claims.
- Venue approval still waiting on host review.
- Wording that pressures a host into a public commitment.
