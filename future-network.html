AI planning roles

Keep AI in service to better questions.

For this repo, AI supports sorting, scenario drafting and pattern recognition. Hosts, organisers and cultural authority still decide what can be announced, trialled or shown.

Useful near-term agents

  • Transport mapper: compares boat, bus and last-return assumptions.
  • Market questioner: turns any venue into a host-review question pack.
  • Notice editor: checks expiry, status, source links and public-safe wording.
  • Media beat sorter: routes confirmed items into markets, music, sport, ferry, arts or community updates.
  • Simulation scribe: records assumptions that a future digital twin could test.

Starter schema

The data packet stays small on purpose.

Enough structure for agents and humans. Enough humility for a real island.

---
title: Possible night market test
status: question gathering only
host_review: pending
location: Straddie Hotel
season_window: winter whale season
contributor_role: projection contributor
visitor_origin_question: local / day-trip / overnight
boat_choice_question: vehicle ferry / Gold Cat / private boat / already on island
simulation_seed:
  crowd_flow: unknown
  last_return: needs timetable check
  projection_surface: needs site walk
  weather_risk: wind and rain
news_network_fit:
  beat: gig guide or weekend wrap
  publish_when: host confirms public details
---
