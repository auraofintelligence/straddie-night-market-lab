Straddie Market Onboarding

A shared intake point for local artists, makers, stallholders, food providers, venues, organisers and helpers who want to join, host or suggest Straddie market activity.

Start by role

Find the doorway that matches your part.

The form collects common market data first, then sorts the follow-up for organisers, venues, stallholders, food providers, artists, media helpers and visitor information.

Hosts and organisers

Share site capacity, dates, bookings, fees, cancellation owner, toilets, power, waste, pack-down and public listing rules.

Stallholders and artists

Register interest with products, photos, socials, stall layout, table or marquee size, site needs, insurance and contact preferences.

Food providers

Record menu, licence or permit needs, allergens, power, gas, cold storage, water, bins, cleanup and queue space.

Venues and local ideas

Suggest a host space, music night add-on, small maker row, food cluster, arts trail stop or community helper role.

Media helpers

Use reviewed records for event listings, public notices, QR updates, weekend wraps and venue-screen copy.

Visitors and locals

Help identify FAQ gaps: times, cost, booking, meals, toilets, accessibility, cash/card, parking, ferry and last return.
