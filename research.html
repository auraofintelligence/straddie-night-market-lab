Findings

What the research suggests.

Point Lookout is the known market baseline

Point Lookout Bowls Club and tourism pages describe local stalls, arts, crafts, food and Sunday or holiday-style daytime markets. A night pattern should learn from this and sit beside it respectfully.

Arts and cultural events already carry stalls

Quandamooka Festival, Straddie Arts Trail and Ubuntu-style gatherings show that artisan, food, music and cultural-program contexts can support market-like layers when led properly.

Venues differ sharply after dark

Straddie Hotel looks more night-capable. Little Ship Club is often more sunset or early-evening. Amity needs careful transport thinking. Dunwich is strongest for gateway data.
