Associated initiative

A market can also be a small Shared Table.

The Shared Table can sit beside a host-led market as a warm, practical layer: gather safely, waste less, feed people, and build the public evidence people choose to share.

Open Shared Table site Open Space Builder

Market roles

The food layer is optional, permissioned and evidence-aware.

It can be one table beside a stall, a community pantry call-out, a shared meal, a surplus rescue lane or a grant-evidence pilot. The first step is a small hosted trial with clear people, place and safety checks.

Gather

What food, tools, prep space, storage and people are actually available this week?

  • What is donated, bought, rescued or grown?
  • What needs cold storage or labelling?
  • Who can say yes to using the space?

Check

What must be kept safe before anything becomes public?

  • Food handling and basic compliance
  • Allergies, expiry, weather and power
  • Cultural, privacy and location restrictions

Share

What can the public noticeboard say while protecting private details?

  • Help needed, with expiry dates
  • Confirmed table times or cancelled notices
  • Simple evidence: meals, waste reduced, volunteers

Sovereign Space Builder

Map the real place before promising the big system.

The useful slice is small: observe what exists, map where it happens, attach safety notes, keep privacy visible, then publish only public-safe learning. The full architecture stays as a compass while the trial stays grounded.

Questions to attach

Every market candidate gets a food and place layer.

Food

  • Is this a snack stall, shared meal, food rescue, produce swap or no-food event?
  • Who checks storage, temperature, labelling and pack-down?
  • What happens to surplus after the market?

Place

  • Where are tables, bins, water, prep zones, paths and power?
  • Which areas are private, sensitive or culturally restricted?
  • Can photos or notes be attached to the place while protecting people?

Evidence

  • What did people contribute?
  • What was safely shared or saved from waste?
  • Which simple evidence could support a grant, partner report or next pilot?
